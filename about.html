Our Story

15 Years of Caring for the Animals You Love

PawCare Veterinary Clinic began in 2011 as a small two-room practice run by Dr. Aditya Sharma, driven by one belief: every pet deserves the same quality of medical care as any family member. Today we've grown into a full-service hospital with a team of 12 specialists, but that founding belief hasn't changed.

From treating a single stray kitten in our first week to caring for over 8,500 pets across the city, our journey has been built on trust, referrals, and the simple act of showing up for animals when they need us most — day or night.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and medically excellent veterinary care that improves the health and happiness of every pet we treat — while supporting and educating the families who love them.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted veterinary clinic in the region — known not just for clinical excellence, but for treating every animal with the warmth and dignity it deserves.
