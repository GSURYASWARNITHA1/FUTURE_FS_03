Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my pet have a check-up?

We recommend an annual wellness exam for healthy adult pets, and twice-yearly visits for puppies, kittens and senior pets.

Do you accept walk-in emergencies?

Yes — our emergency line operates 24/7 and walk-ins are always seen, though calling ahead helps us prepare.

What should I bring to my pet's first visit?

Please bring any previous medical records, current medications, and a stool sample if requested for parasite screening.

Do you offer payment plans for surgery?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options for major procedures — speak to our front desk team for details.
